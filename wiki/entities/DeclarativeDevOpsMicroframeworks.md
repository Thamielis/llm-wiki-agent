[[DeclarativeDevOpsMicroframeworks]] is the article series that ties the current wiki together. Its main through-line is that PowerShell codebases scale better when teams use declarative models, single-purpose reusable frameworks, strong module boundaries, and automation patterns that are validated and idempotent by construction.
