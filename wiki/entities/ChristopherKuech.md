Christopher Kuech is the cited author of [[Functional Programming in PowerShell]] and the broader [[DeclarativeDevOpsMicroframeworks]] series. In this source, he advocates a pragmatic functional style for [[PowerShell]] that emphasizes purity, higher-order functions, and reduced side effects.
