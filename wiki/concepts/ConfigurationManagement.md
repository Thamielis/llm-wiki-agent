[[ConfigurationManagement]] is framed as the problem of generating and maintaining the right static configuration for each environment or cluster set without drifting into duplicated, inconsistent files. The current wiki emphasizes formal domain modeling and declarative merging of partial configs over heavyweight platform-first solutions.
