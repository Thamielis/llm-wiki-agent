[[HigherOrderFunctions]] are functions that take functions as inputs or return functions as outputs. The source treats `ForEach-Object`, `Where-Object`, `Group-Object`, `Sort-Object`, jobs with callbacks, and reduce-style helpers like `Compress-Object` as the practical PowerShell forms of this idea.
