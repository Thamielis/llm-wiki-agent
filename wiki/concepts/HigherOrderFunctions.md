[[HigherOrderFunctions]] are functions that take other functions as inputs or return them as outputs. In the current PowerShell sources, the idea is mostly expressed through pipeline commands such as `ForEach-Object`, `Where-Object`, `Group-Object`, and `Sort-Object`, which let data transformations stay concise and composable.
