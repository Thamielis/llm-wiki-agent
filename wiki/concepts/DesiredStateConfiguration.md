[[DesiredStateConfiguration]] refers to PowerShell DSC, the built-in desired-state framework used as both example and contrast in the current wiki. It is praised for idempotence, validation, and self-healing, but criticized for being too asynchronous and Windows-focused for several DevOps scenarios.
