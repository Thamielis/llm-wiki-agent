## Summary
This German-language roundup lists recommended PowerShell module and script templates, focusing on repository structure, CI/CD integration, analyzers, tests, and scaffolding utilities. It is not a theoretical article but a curated set of references for starting new modules or automation repositories quickly. In the wiki, it functions as a survey of available template ecosystems and common scaffold features.

## Key Claims
- PowerShell module templates are often differentiated by their degree of CI/CD, testing, and documentation automation.
- Templates commonly integrate tools such as GitHub Actions, Pester, PSScriptAnalyzer, Plaster, PlatyPS, and Invoke-Build.
- Different templates trade off simplicity for automation and extensibility.
